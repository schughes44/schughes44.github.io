The Evolution of a Muscle Car

Evolution

Throughout the decades, muscle cars have changed in style, performance and comfort. Below is a table that shows an example of when the body style changed throughout the production of the Chevrolet Camaro. This table displays how long each generation of Camaro lasted and when a new body style was born. Also, you can see the gap in production between 2002 and 2010. Chevrolet decided to revive the beloved Camaro after the not so popluar reviews of the Fourth Generation in 2010. This new look brought a classic back to life with a new, sleek style.

First Generation 1967 1969
Second Generation 1970 1981
Third Generation 1982 1992
Fourth Generation 1993 2002
