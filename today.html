Today's Muscle Cars

After the surge of production of muscle cars during the 60's and 70's, companies realized the the popularity and continued to produce them with changes over the years. Whether it be cosmetic or performance, the name and quality of the muscle car stayed the same. Even comies including Chevrolet and Dodge, began to reproduce new muscles cars in the name of the classics that have since been discontinued.

Chevrolet Camaro

You can see the simialrites in the body style of back in 1969 to today. Chevrolet reintroduced this new body style back in 2010 to mirror the look of arguably the best year of the Camaro.

Dodge Challenger

Like the Camaro, Dodge reintroduced the Challenger to its line taking styles from the earlier model of challenger. Even with the modern styles of the vehicle, you can still see the simularities.
